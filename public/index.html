About Sticker Collector 2018

App Description


The World Cup 2018 is here, and so is the sticker album! This is why we have developed an app that will help you keep track of every single one of your stickers, so you don’t have to worry about anything, but collecting them! Here you will find the different sections that you have in the album, such as Specials, Host Cities, Stadiums, and more. You will also be able to add and remove your repeated stickers, so you can be in control. Don’t be left behind, and start collecting and registering your stickers today!
